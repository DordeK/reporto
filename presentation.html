The Solution

End-to-End Invoice Intelligence

A fully automated pipeline: ingest structured Peppol invoices, parse them into a queryable database, run AI anomaly detection, and surface exceptions in a clean dashboard. Finance teams review only what matters — everything else is approved automatically.

📡

Peppol Ingestion

Pulls UBL 2.1 XML invoices from the mock Peppol network provider. Standard e-invoicing format used across all EU mandates.

UBL 2.1 · ISO 20022
🔍

Smart Parser

Extracts all structured fields — supplier, customer, line items, VAT subtotals, due dates, payment terms — into normalised PostgreSQL rows.

🤖

AI Anomaly Engine

LLM acts as a senior AP auditor. Detects duplicates, VAT mismatches, impossible dates, missing IDs, and reverse-charge violations.

📊

Live Dashboard

Finance teams see flagged exceptions with plain-language explanations and severity ratings. Approve clean invoices in bulk.

Features

Everything a Finance Team Needs

📥

One-Click Peppol Ingestion

Click "Fetch from Provider" — the backend pulls all pending invoices from the Peppol network, parses every XML field, and persists them in under 2 seconds.

🔎

Deep Invoice Inspection

Full parsed detail view for every invoice: all line items, tax subtotals, supplier & customer details, and payment terms. Nothing hidden.

🧠

LLM-Powered Audit

The model reasons across all invoices simultaneously — catching cross-invoice patterns like duplicates that rule-based systems miss entirely.

📋

Plain-Language Explanations

Each anomaly comes with a plain-language explanation of what's wrong, why it matters, and what action to take. No technical jargon for finance staff.

🔬

Belgian VAT Reports

Generates Belgian VAT return summaries with grid-level breakdown — ready to feed directly into Intervat or your ERP system.

📜

Immutable Audit Log

Every action — fetch, anomaly detection run, manual override — is logged with timestamp and user. Full compliance trail for auditors and regulators.

📤

Manual Upload Support

Upload UBL 2.1 XML files directly via drag-and-drop. Works for invoices that arrive outside the Peppol network — email attachments, portal downloads.

⚡

AI Report Chat

Ask natural language questions about your invoice data — "show me all invoices from TechSupplier with VAT issues this quarter" — and get instant answers.
