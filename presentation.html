Reporto

AI-powered e-invoice intelligence · Belgian e-Invoicing Hackathon 2026

Reporto ingests Peppol UBL 2.1 invoices, runs 10 deterministic anomaly checks, and lets finance teams generate compliance reports by typing plain English — no SQL required.

You ask a question. An LLM turns it into a validated ReportDefinition JSON, which is compiled to parameterised SQL, EXPLAIN-checked, executed, and returned with a chart and an AI narrative. The security model blocks injection at every step.
